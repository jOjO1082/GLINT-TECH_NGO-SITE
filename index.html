About Glint Tech

Too many talented young Nigerians are ready to work, but don’t have access to the right skills, guidance, tools, or professional networks. Glint Tech exists to change that — by delivering practical training that leads to real opportunities.

Our training is designed for beginners and growth-minded learners. We teach with real tools, real projects and real mentorship — so graduates don’t just “learn”; they become employable and ready to earn.

Mission, Vision & Values

We’re not just teaching tools — we’re building confidence, competence, and a clear path to income.

Our Mission

To provide free, world-class digital skills training and mentorship that helps young Nigerians become employable, freelance-ready, and economically empowered — regardless of background or location.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every motivated young person can access digital education, build a future-proof career, and earn sustainably — from anywhere.

Our Values

  • Access: learning should not depend on income.
  • Excellence: we teach with real standards, not shortcuts.
  • Integrity: we deliver what we promise.
  • Community: nobody grows alone.

Programs (Income-Ready Skills)

Each track is designed to take you from beginner to confident, with projects you can show to clients and employers. We focus on practical assignments, feedback, and a portfolio-first approach.

Graphic Design

Learn branding, flyers, social media design, typography, and portfolio projects that help you pitch confidently.

UI/UX Design

Learn user research, wireframing, prototyping, and building modern interfaces with Figma-ready projects.

Web Development

Build responsive websites, learn JavaScript fundamentals, and ship real projects you can host and share.

Digital Marketing

Learn content strategy, social media growth, basic SEO, and how to manage clients professionally.

Video Editing

Learn storytelling, editing basics, captions, social formats, and build a reel that attracts paying work.

Freelancing & Career Prep

Learn pricing, proposals, client communication, and how to present your work with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions we get from applicants and supporters.

Is the training really free?

Yes. Our training is offered free through donations, volunteer support, and community partnerships.

Do I need a laptop to apply?

It helps, especially for online learning. If you don’t have one, apply anyway — we also support physical sessions when available.

How long is each program?

Program length depends on the track and cohort schedule. We always prioritize practical learning and portfolio delivery over rushing.

Do I get mentorship?

Yes. Mentorship is built into the learning process through reviews, feedback, and guided project delivery.

Is it online or physical?

Both. Depending on location and resources, cohorts may be online, physical, or hybrid.

How can I support Glint Tech?

You can donate, volunteer as a mentor, sponsor data/equipment, or connect us to organizations that want to train or hire talent.
