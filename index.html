Glint Tech is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing free tech training to young adults and children. Our mission is to empower the next generation with the skills they need to succeed in the digital world, enabling them to earn from home and excel in their early careers.

How it Works: Your Journey to a Tech-Powered Future

We’ve made it simple and accessible for anyone—regardless of background, education, or experience—to start learning tech skills and earning from home. Here’s how you can begin your journey with us in just three easy steps:

Step one

Register for Free

No fees, no barriers, just your willingness to learn

Signing up is easy! Click on the “Join the Training” button and fill out a short form with your name, email, phone number, and preferred training program.

Who Can Apply?

  • Young adults looking to gain digital skill and improve career prospects
  • Children interested in learning technology from an early age
  • Individuals who want to start freelancing or working remotely
  • Anyone eager to learn, regardless of educational background

What You'll Get After Registration:

Access to our training materials and live sessions

Guidance from experienced mentors and industry professionals

A supportive learning community of like-minded students and professionals

Once registered, you’ll receive a confirmation email or SMS with details about the next training session in your local government area or online.

Join the Training

Step two

Join a Training Session

Turn your new skill into real income opportunities

We conduct our training both in-person and online, so you can choose the method that works best for you.

Training Formats:

  • Physical Workshops

    If you’re in an area where we’re hosting a session, you can join us for an immersive, hands-on training experience. We bring laptops, internet access, and expert instructors to ensure you get the best learning environment.

  • Online Learnings

    Can’t attend in person? No problem! Our live virtual sessions and recorded lessons allow you to learn at your own pace, with support from our instructors whenever you need help.

You’ll work on real-life projects, receive feedback from experienced mentors, and build your own portfolio while you learn.

Join the Training

Step three

Start Learning & Earning

Hands-on, practical training designed for beginners and professionals alike.

Learning with us isn’t just about theory—it’s about taking action and building a future for yourself.

What Happens After Training?

  • Freelancing Guidance – We’ll show you how to set up profiles on platforms like Fiverr and Upwork,
  • Portfolio Development – You’ll create a personal portfolio to showcase your work to potential
  • Job Opportunities – We connect outstanding students with companies looking for digital talent.
  • Continued Mentorship – Even after your training, you’ll be part of a community where you can ask questions, get feedback, and stay motivated.
